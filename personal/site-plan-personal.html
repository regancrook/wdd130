Overview

Purpose

Have you ever been to a uninviting birthday party, for your kid? Have you ever needed to get things done around the house, but your kid kept walking around saying they were bouncing off the walls? I have a website that rents out inflatables and games to keep that birthday party exciting. It’ll have your kid outside all day, so you can finally clean your kitchen. We have a wide variety, that even the adults will be entertained! Are you ready to have a sparkling clean house, and a tired out happy kid? Get them something they can ACTUALLY bounce on!

Audience

My intended audience is everyone! So many people, all ages, can enjoy a bounce house or slide. Most people have kids in their lives. This can be their own children, cousin, neice, nephew, friend, or sibling. Everyone has a birthday, that an inflatable would be the best addition to have at their party.

FAQs

How safe is it to rent an inflatable? Please refer to all of the instructions and guidelines listed on your rental contract and on the unit itself. It is important that all users follow the rules listed on the contract and the unit. The inflatables are all safety-rated and exceed industry safety standards. This means that they are built with safety in mind, using the latest in high-security designs and fire retardant materials. How many people will your inflatables hold? The maximum number of riders of each group that should play in a bounce house is 6-8 kids, that are the same size. No big kids with small kids. Will the Inflatable harm my lawn? We cannot guarantee that the inflatable will not affect your lawn. In most cases, it will only temporarily flatten the grass, which should recover in a few days. When renting a waterslide, damage can occur to the lawn in the area around the slide itself. This is primarily the result of water getting under the tarp and a high amount of foot traffic in one concentrated area. What type of power is required for an inflatable? The inflatable should be within 100ft. of a 110v outlet, on it's own 15-20 amp circuit. We will supply the power cord, but we do ask that nothing else be plugged into the outlet being used. Some of our larger inflatables might need multiple outlets on separate circuits.
